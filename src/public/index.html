About

TurboTravel, product of TurboTech Innovations, LLC, is a cost-effective mobility guidance application that provides an elegant solution to a modern traveling problem. In the past, we have found that, to find the most cost-effective means of travel, we often spend countless hours, scouring different apps and websites for the best prices, whether it be for ride-hailing or air travel. With Turbo Travel, you simply enter your start and end destination address, and you will be shown the real-time estimated price of UBER and LYFT side-by-side, allowing you to choose the best travel method. Turbo Travel has also been developed to detect and price compare for long-distance travel.

If your travel trip is accessible by flight, then you will be provided with the cheapest airfare currently available, along with any important flight details (flight date, Airline name, start/destination airport names). Based on your start and destination addresses, the closest airports will also be provided. To further help you experience the smoothest and hassle-free travel experience, we even provide you a link to book your flight!
