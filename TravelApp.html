About

APP-NAME provides an elegant solution to a modern traveling problem. To find the most cost-effective means of travel, we often spend countless minutes, sometimes hours, scouring different apps/websites for the best prices, whether it be for ride-hailing or air travel.
With APP-NAME, you simply input your start address and your destination address and you will be shown the real-time estimated price of UBER and LYFT side-by-side, allowing you to choose the most cost-efficient travel method. If your trip is greater than 100 miles, APP-NAME will automatically provide you with the cheapest airfare ticket, along with the Airline name. APP-NAME has also been developed to handle long-distance travel as well. When your travel trip is greater than 100 miles, APP-NAME automatically grabs the nearest airport of your starting location as well as the nearest airport to your destination location. From there, APP-NAME seamlessly provides you with the cheapest Airfare, along with providing you with the airline, starting/destination airport, and the respective link to the Airline's website, all for ease of booking.
